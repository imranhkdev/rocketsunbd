About Us

Since 2017, we have successfully developed several business organizations across trading and manufacturing sectors. Our journey began with the establishment of Merckallyrise International Limited in 2017 to support trading and indenting businesses. As part of our strategic expansion, we launched Rocket Sun BD in 2021. In 2023, focusing on pharmaceutical packaging materials such as Alu-Alu, PVC, and PVDC, we entered the local manufacturing sector through Fair Aluminum Packaging Ltd. In 2024, we further strengthened our presence in the pharmaceutical industry by establishing Aroma Life Science Ltd. With each milestone, we have remained committed to delivering quality, innovation, and reliability across all our ventures. Our growing portfolio reflects a vision built on strategic diversification and a deep understanding of industry needs. Today, we stand as a trusted partner for businesses in the pharmaceutical, veterinary, food, and flexible packaging industries — and we continue to look forward with ambition and purpose.

Mission: Our mission is to establish a strong global presence by delivering innovative solutions and building sustainable partnerships across diverse industries. We are committed to creating exceptional value for our clients, employees, and stakeholders while promoting responsible business practices that benefit both society and the environment. As part of our international expansion plan, we aim to establish strategic branches in Guangzhou, China; Singapore; and Dubai, along with a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Saudi Arabia.

Vision: Our vision is to be a globally recognized organization known for excellence, integrity, and innovation. We aim to be the first choice for our customers by delivering high-quality and reliable solutions. Through continuous innovation and strategic expansion, we strive to strengthen our global presence and establish operations in major cities worldwide

Work Process

Complete workflow of RocketSunBD from start to finish

01

Sourcing & Procurement

APIs, excipients, and packaging materials are ordered from China, India, and Thailand based on demand.
Customs clearance and import processes are completed using IRC and indenting licenses.
Payments and financial transactions are handled via LC/TT through Trust Bank, City Bank, and Dutch Bangla Bank.
02

Manufacturing & Packaging

Alu-Alu, PVC, and PVDC foils are produced at Fair Aluminum Packaging Ltd following defined quality standards.
Printed/non-printed foils, vials, bottles, and sprayers go through strict quality control inspection before dispatch.
Local stock and imported materials are packed and labeled to pharmaceutical-grade standards for delivery.
03

Sales & Client Service

Client requirements from pharma, veterinary, and food industries are analyzed to prepare tailored product offers.
Products are delivered and after-sales support is provided through the Aroma Life Science and Merckallyrise network.
Long-term partnerships and new market expansion are managed through coordination between the Dhaka and China offices.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our products, import process, and business services.

What types of industries do you serve, and what products do you supply?

We serve a wide range of industries including Pharmaceuticals, Veterinary, Food, and Flexible Packaging sectors. Our product portfolio covers three major categories — Packaging Materials (such as PVC, PVDC, Aluminium Foil, Vials, Bottles, Sprayers, and Applicators), Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) including Antibiotics, Anticancer, Antidiabetic, Hormones, and more, along with Excipients and Solvents like IPA, Ethanol, Methanol, and Acetone — all available through local stock or import.

Do you manufacture products locally, or do you also import from abroad?

We operate both ways. Through our own manufacturing facility — Fair Aluminum Packaging Ltd — we locally produce high-quality Aluminium Foils and PVC/PVDC films. Additionally, we import a wide range of packaging materials, APIs, Excipients, and Solvents from globally trusted sources in countries like Thailand, India, and China, ensuring consistent quality and competitive pricing for our clients.

How can I source pharmaceutical APIs or Excipients through your company?

We offer both local stock availability and indenting services for pharmaceutical APIs and Excipients. Whether you need common Excipients like MCC, HPMC, Lactose, or Mannitol, or specialized APIs from therapeutic categories like Ophthalmic, Respiratory, or Oncology — our team can source them efficiently. Simply reach out to us with your requirements, and we will provide the best available options in terms of quality, regulatory compliance, and delivery timelines.
